The Challenge

  • African businesses struggle to find verified international buyers

A comprehensive digital platform that connects verified African exporters with international buyers through:

  • Trade Intelligence — Real-time market insights and analytics
  • AI Trade Advisor — AI-powered assistant for trade guidance
